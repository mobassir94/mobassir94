Bengali.AI Handwritten Grapheme Classification

The task of this competition was to Classify the components of handwritten Bengali graphemes from given images. Each word is composed of three pieces; a (1) Grapheme root (which confusingly are actually vowels, consonants, and consonant conjuncts themselves), a (2) vowel diacritic, and a (3) consonant diacritic. There are 168 different roots, 11 different v-diacritics, and 7 different c-diacritics total over train and test. Our job is to find and detect these pieces in words. The test set contains words not present in the train's 1292 words (but uses the same pieces).
